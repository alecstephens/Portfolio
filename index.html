Background

I've always known that I wanted to have a career that had a focus on technology.

However, it wasn't until midway through highschool that I realized that I wanted this career to involve programming.

I took one year of computer science at a local community college, and I decided that was enough college for me.

I then started a TechDegree course with Team Treehouse, which allowed me to work more along with studying.

I eventually finished the course, and now I spend a lot of my time building various programming projects, along with working full-time.
